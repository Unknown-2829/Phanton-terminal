# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or insults
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct which could be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [@unknownlll2829](https://t.me/unknownlll2829).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, resulting in a response deemed necessary and appropriate.
